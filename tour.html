Controls

There is a number of ways to navigate the 360 Tour!

Side Bar

The side bar can be seen as soon as the tour is loaded.
The side bar is split into the categories of sorting and can be quickly hopped around through
The side bar is also collapsable by pressing the little X in the top right corner of the side bar. This can be uncollapsed when by pressing on the Hamburger

Mini Map

The mini map is opened up by pressing on the center + button. This opens up a Short Cuts UI
Pressing the Map Icon will load up a Floor Plan to which you can change Camera Locations

Chevron

This is the most "Default" way to move around and its by pressing on the Arrow that appears on the floor
this will move between the camera positions much like moving on Google Maps Street View
