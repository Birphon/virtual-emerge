Controls

Camera Movement

Movement around the tour is done by two methods depending on your device:
For PC Users you need to click and drag to move around the Camera. Zooming in and out is done by the mouse wheel.
For Mobile Users you need to swipe around for Camera movement. Zooming in and out is done by the normal mobile gestures of Pinching and Expanding.

Side Bar

The side bar can be seen as soon as the tour is loaded. The side bar is split into the categories of sorting and can be quickly hopped around through
The side bar is also collapsable by pressing the little X in the top right corner of the side bar. This can be uncollapsed when by pressing on the Hamburger

Button Movement

On the boards are buttons that can be found. These are clickable and will take you to the set destination. This is the alternative movement to the Side Bar
